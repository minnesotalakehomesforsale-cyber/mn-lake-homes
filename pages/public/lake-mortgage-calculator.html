Don't forget the lakefront-specific costs

  • Septic & well: most lake homes are on private septic — a failing system can run well into five figures. Budget for inspection and possible replacement.
  • Dock, lift & shoreline: docks, boat lifts, and riprap/erosion work add up, and some need permits.
  • Insurance: waterfront, and any flood-zone exposure, can push premiums above a typical home.
  • Seasonal carrying costs: road-association dues, winterizing, and second-home utilities.
