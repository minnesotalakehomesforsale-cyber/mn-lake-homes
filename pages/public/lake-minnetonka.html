The Ultimate
Boating Lifestyle

Lake Minnetonka isn't just a body of water; it's Minnesota's premier social and recreational playground. From highly coveted transient slips at Lord Fletcher's to the elite private docks of Wayzata bay, the lake offers a sprawling interconnected network of deep-water access.

Owning a lake home here guarantees you front-row VIP access to the spectacular 4th of July fireworks, quiet morning paddle-boarding coves, and world-class walleye fishing exactly 30 feet from your back patio.

Trophy Real Estate
and Architecture

The housing inventory on Lake Minnetonka represents the pinnacle of architectural excellence in the Midwest. From historic, generational estates gracefully overlooking Gray's Bay, to ultra-modern glass architectural marvels nestled secretly along the shoreline of Orono, the options are spectacular.

  • Wayzata & Orono: Unmatched luxury and top-tier school districts.
  • Excelsior: Historic downtown charm and highly walkable boutique shopping.
  • Minnetonka Beach: Extreme exclusivity and flat, sandy shorelines.

A Year-Round
Destination

Lake Minnetonka isn't a summer postcard — it's a four-season neighborhood. Summer weekends bring sailing regattas, pontoon parties, and standing-room patios at Lord Fletcher's and 6Smith. Fall delivers stunning shoreline color and some of the best lakefront walking trails in the state.

Winter transforms the lake into a proper ice town: skating rinks, the Art Shanty Projects, ice houses, and pond hockey tournaments you can walk to from your door. And come spring, the docks go back in before the ink dries on the calendar. Owning here means membership in a community that actually uses every month of the year.
