**Goal: reach "tiered-complete" by end of Month 6.**
_Owner: Director of Marketing · Trunk metric: qualified leads/month from organic._

## The finish line (what "done" means)

By end of Month 6 (Dec 2026):

- **All 15 Tier-1 lakes + top ~10 Tier-1 towns fully built** — all 30 applicable angles each (~25 pages × 30 ≈ **750 blogs**).
- **Every other money page seeded** — 1-2 blogs each so all ~200 pages feed their "From the blog" module (~**260 blogs**).
- **Evergreen / trust set + state pillars** published (~**15-20 blogs**) linking down into the network.
- **Market reports running** — quarterly per Tier-1 page, two cycles in the window (~**50 blogs**).

**Total ≈ 1,050-1,100 high-quality, lake-specific posts.** Achievable at a ramped, quality-first pace. The literal "all 30 on all 200" (~6,000) is the Months 7-18 vision, not this window.

## Cadence — ramp, don't redline

The site's money pages are already live and good, so the only thing that needs to get indexed now is the blogs. Start slow to lock the template and confirm Google is indexing, then scale.

| Month | Pace (per weekday) | Blogs that month | Focus |
|---|---|---|---|
| **1** (Jun-Jul) | 3/day | ~65 | Foundation: lock template + workflow, prove indexing |
| **2** (Jul-Aug) | 5/day | ~110 | Tier-1 lake depth + Tier-1 town cores |
| **3** (Aug-Sep) | 8/day | ~175 | Tier-1 completion push + Q3 market reports |
| **4** (Sep-Oct) | 10/day | ~220 | Finish all Tier-1 (30 each) + begin long-tail seeding |
| **5** (Oct-Nov) | 10/day | ~220 | Seed all remaining ~175 pages (1-2 each) |
| **6** (Nov-Dec) | 10/day | ~220 | Finish seeding + Q4 market reports + state pillars + refresh |
| | **Total** | **~1,010-1,100** | |

**The one hard rule:** never publish faster than blogs get indexed, and the moment two posts feel interchangeable, slow down. Thin duplicates at volume is the only thing Google actually penalizes.

## Month-by-month

### Month 1 — Foundation (~65 blogs)
- Finalize the blog template + production workflow off the 30-angle library.
- Publish the **6 evergreen/trust blogs** ("Why a local specialist beats a national portal," etc.) — these anchor positioning and link down everywhere.
- Build the **3 core angles for all 15 Tier-1 lakes** (buyer guide #8, lifestyle #1/#7, comparison #26) = 45 posts.
- **Outcome:** every Tier-1 lake has 3+ blogs live, template proven, GSC indexing confirmed, baseline metrics captured.

### Month 2 — Tier-1 depth + town cores (~110 blogs)
- Push Tier-1 lakes from 3 → ~10 angles each (recreation, ownership, seasonal).
- Build the **3 core angles for top 10 Tier-1 towns** (#28-30).
- **Outcome:** Tier-1 lakes mid-depth; all Tier-1 towns seeded; first long-tail rankings appearing.

### Month 3 — Tier-1 completion push (~175 blogs)
- Drive Tier-1 lakes toward all 30 applicable angles.
- Publish **Q3 market reports** for Tier-1 lakes/towns (#23).
- **Outcome:** Tier-1 lakes ~80% complete; recurring freshness engine live.

### Month 4 — Finish Tier-1 + start the tail (~220 blogs)
- Complete all 30 on every Tier-1 lake + town (~750-blog milestone hit).
- Begin seeding the long tail: 1 blog on the highest-traffic non-Tier-1 pages.
- **Outcome:** Tier-1 fully built and ranking; long-tail seeding underway.

### Month 5 — Seed everything (~220 blogs)
- 1-2 seed blogs on every remaining money page so all ~200 feed their module.
- **Outcome:** zero empty "From the blog" modules across the site.

### Month 6 — Close out + freshen (~220 blogs)
- Finish remaining seeds + **Q4 market reports** + state pillar pages ("Best MN Lakes for Families," etc.).
- Refresh/upgrade any Tier-1 posts underperforming in GSC.
- **Outcome:** tiered-complete. Every page seeded, Tier-1 fully built, freshness + pillars in place.

## Production system (the templatable part)

Per blog, non-negotiable:
- 1,200-1,800 words, **one** angle, genuinely lake/town-specific (real towns, fish, prices, seasons).
- **Tagged** to its lake/town (auto-fills the page's "From the blog" module).
- **Dense backlinks — 8-12 contextual links per post** across four buckets, per `Internal Link & Backlink Standard.md` (source of truth): 3-5 UP to money pages, 3-4 ACROSS to other blogs, 1-2 to the get-matched/agents flow, and 2-4 OUT to reputable non-competitor external authority sources. (This replaces the old "2-3 up + 1-2 related" rule.) Internal links may only point to pages in the CONFIRMED LIVE section of `Live Money Pages Registry.md`; outbound URLs must be verified to resolve and must never be competitors.
- Title with the place name in the first 60 chars, meta description (145-160 chars), slug `/blog/[place]/[topic]`.

**Batch for efficiency:** research a lake once, then draft several angles from that research pass. Weekly: publish the batch, check indexing, internal-link the new posts together. Monthly: ship the market report + review rankings, double down on what's ranking.

## Risks & guardrails
- **Duplication penalty** — the real risk at volume. Mitigation: tier the depth, skip non-applicable angles, keep every post place-specific.
- **Indexing lag** — young domain. Mitigation: ramp slowly Month 1, watch GSC, lean on internal links over raw volume if pages stall.
- **Phase 1** — organic only, no paid promotion. Consumer-side voice only; no agent-recruiting content (that's Agent Growth's lane).
- **Claims** — "vetted, licensed, local, matched," never "unbiased" or "best agent."
