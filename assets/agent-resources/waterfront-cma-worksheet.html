Method 2 — Price per front foot (weight: 30%)

On many lakes, frontage is the single biggest driver. Take recent sales' $ / front ft, adjust for quality, multiply by this lot's frontage.

Rule of thumb Premium sand + west exposure can command 1.5–2× the $/ft of a muck-bottom, tree-shaded north lot on the same lake. Never average blindly.

Method 3 — Land (lot) value floor (weight: 20%)

What would this sell for as vacant buildable lakeshore? On tear-down candidates this can be the real value. Compare to recent vacant-lot sales on the lake.
